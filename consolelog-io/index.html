Project Details

I have found that one of the best ways to learn something is to actually teach. It is easy for me to say that I know something, but the real test of knowledge and comprehension comes when I have to explain it to others. As I learn new things or find solutions to difficult problems, I write a blog post with everything that I learned, in the hopes to help someone else avoid a similar problem, and to help myself avoid the same problem in the future.
